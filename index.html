Technical Due Diligence

For investors, acquiring companies, or organizations evaluating major AI vendor partnerships, assessing the true capabilities of an AI technology is critical. Our technical due diligence provides rigorous, objective evaluation of AI systems, underlying algorithms, and data architectures.

We look beyond the marketing claims to assess the technical viability, scalability, and defendability of the AI assets. Our deep scientific background and extensive commercial experience enable us to identify hidden technical debts, assess integration challenges, and provide clear, actionable risk assessments that inform critical investment and acquisition decisions.
